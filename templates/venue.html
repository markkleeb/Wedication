Senate Garage

4 N Front St

Kingston, NY 12401


Senate Garage, built in the 1920s, was a parking garage where chauffeur-driven automobiles parked in the bustling Uptown Kingston.

Senate Garage was transformed into a unique place that still celebrates its history. We hope you enjoy this special space as much as we do!

The wedding will be catered by Bridgecreek Catering, an award-winning farm-to-table caterer. They will provide Kosher, gluten-free, and vegan options.








Directions

Driving Directions!

Senate Garage is located about two hours north of New York City. You can also take Metro North on the Hudson Line to Poughkeepsie and then transfer to the KPL-NB train to Kingston (approx 45 min)



Hotels & Parking

We have a block of rooms at the Best Western Plus located at 503 Washington Avenue. Tell them you are part of the Kleback/Gross wedding. With our discount, rooms are $129/night. There's a pool, so bring a bathing suit.

There is metered street parking and municipal parking in several locations throughout uptown Kingston (meters up till 6pm). There is a large parking lot 1 block away.
